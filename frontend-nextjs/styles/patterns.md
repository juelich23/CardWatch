### Primary
```html

```

### Secondary
```html
className="bg-panel-2 hover:bg-hover text-text border border-border px-4 py-2 rounded-md transition-colors focus:outline-none focus:ring-2 focus:ring-ring disabled:bg-disabled disabled:text-muted disabled:cursor-not-allowed"
```

### Ghost
